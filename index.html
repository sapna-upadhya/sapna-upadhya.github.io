About me

I am a UX designer with a business analysis background focused on creating beautiful experiences through designs. I offer significant experience in understanding business needs, communication with clients/stakeholders, user research, market analysis and project management.

My previous experience has taught me the importance of understanding the needs and goals of the business, voice of the customer, thinking from different perspectives and time management. I strongly believe that these skills are the pillars for my UX career and they will help me in solving UX problems through designing for the users with empathy.

At the moment I am working as a UX designer intern at a Berlin-based startup and also working as a freelancer on a short-term project. I would love to join a team as a full-time UX designer to showcase my user research, information architecture, and interface designing skills that can solve user interface problems. I am authorized to work in Germany and ready to start immediately.
